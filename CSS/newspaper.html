WHO WILL WIN WTC 2025

The ICC World Test Championship (WTC) is a biennial cricket tournament organised by the International Cricket Council (ICC). It is played over two years and is the premier championship for Test cricket.[1] Australia are the current champions, having defeated India in the 2023 final. India have played in each WTC final, finishing runners-up in both. The WTC league games are organized by the host nation's cricketing board, whereas the WTC finals is organized directly by the ICC. The inaugural ICC World Test Championship started with the 2019 Ashes series and finished with New Zealand lifting the trophy after defeating India in the final in June 2021. The second ICC World Test Championship started on 4 August 2021 with the Pataudi Trophy series[2] and finished with Australia lifting the trophy after defeating India in the final in June 2023. The third ICC World Test Championship started on 16 June 2023 with the 2023 Ashes series and will conclude with the final in England in the summer of 2025. The 2 teams qualified for wtc final are Australia and South Africa.The ICC World Test Championship (WTC) Final for the 2023–2025 cycle is scheduled to take place at Lord's Cricket Ground in London from June 11 to June 15, 2025. The final will feature South Africa and Australia, both of whom have secured their spots following strong performances in the league stage. South Africa confirmed their place by defeating Pakistan in Centurion, marking their first appearance in a WTC Final. Australia's recent successes, including a significant win in Melbourne, have solidified their position as the second finalist. This match will determine the champion of the third edition of the ICC World Test Championship, which began in June 2023. The final is set to commence at 10:00 AM GMT (11:00 AM local time) on June 11, 2025.
